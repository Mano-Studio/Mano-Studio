Osteria Nora

Handmade pasta. Natural wine. Tuesday through Sunday.

Reserve a table

Nora opened in 2019 in a ground-floor space on Carrer del Rec. The walls are old stone. The tables are close together. We make pasta every morning and write the menu around whatever came in from the market.

We work with small producers from Catalonia and Italy. The wine list is short and changes often. Reservations are recommended. Walk-ins when we have space.

dedicated to the enjoyment of eating and drinking.

In the kitchen

Marco Ferretti

Marco is the architect of our evenings. A chef who prefers the shadows of the kitchen to the spotlight of the dining room. His philosophy is simple: the ingredient is the hero, the wine is the companion, and the clock doesn't exist once you walk through our doors.
