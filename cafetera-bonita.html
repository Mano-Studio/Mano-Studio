How We Do It

01 Direct Sourcing

We buy direct from farms we've visited. Every bean has a name, a place, and a farmer behind it.

02 Roasted in Barcelona

Our roasting partner SlowMov handles every batch a few streets away. Fresh, traceable, and seasonal.

03 Served with Care

No automatics. Every drink is pulled by hand. We take the time because you deserve a coffee worth sitting down for.
